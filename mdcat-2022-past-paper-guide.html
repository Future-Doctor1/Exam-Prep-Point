What Was the MDCAT 2022 Exam?

MDCAT 2022 was the national Medical and Dental College Admission Test held for admission to MBBS and BDS programs across Pakistan for the 2022 academic session. Like other years, it tested candidates on the FSc Pre-Medical syllabus — Biology, Chemistry, Physics, and English — as the primary entry requirement into public and private medical and dental colleges nationwide, alongside provincial execution partners such as UHS Lahore for Punjab.

Background: A Turbulent Year for MDCAT

2022 is remembered by many aspirants as one of the more disrupted MDCAT cycles, with reports at the time of irregularities and administrative issues around the national test that led to significant public and media attention, delays, and follow-up action by the then-regulatory body. Because the exact details (dates, retests, and investigation outcomes) were subject to official clarifications after the fact, aspirants researching this year should treat this article as a general study guide rather than a legal or administrative record, and should verify any specific historical claims about MDCAT 2022 directly with the Pakistan Medical and Dental Council (PMDC), which now oversees the exam.
