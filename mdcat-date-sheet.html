As of July 1, 2026: MDCAT 2026 registration is open. The regular-fee deadline is July 8, 2026; late registration (with extra fee) closes July 13, 2026. The exam itself is on Sunday, August 16, 2026. There is no live "MDCAT 2025" registration anymore — that cycle concluded last year.

02 — FEE & DOCUMENTS

What registration actually costs

Candidate TypeRegular FeeLate Fee
Pakistan test centresPKR 9,000PKR 13,000
International centre (Riyadh)PKR 45,000PKR 55,000

All fees are non-refundable and non-transferable — pay only once you're certain of your test centre, since it cannot be changed after selection.

Documents required at registration:

  • 01CNIC or B-Form — required for all candidates.
  • 02Juvenile Registration Certificate (JRC) — a new requirement for candidates under 18 on the exam date, issued by NADRA, in addition to the B-Form.
  • 03Recent passport-sized photograph — per PMDC's upload specifications.
  • 04HSSC / FSc (Pre-Medical) marks certificate or equivalent — minimum 60% required, with Biology and Chemistry as mandatory subjects.
  • 05IBCC equivalence certificate — only for candidates with foreign qualifications (e.g. O/A Levels).

05 — A NOTE ON ACCURACY

PMDC can change the schedule

Important

PMDC has explicitly stated it reserves the right to amend the MDCAT schedule if required — exactly what happened in 2025 when floods pushed the exam back three weeks. Admit-card and result dates above are historical-pattern estimates, not official announcements. Always confirm directly at pmdc.pk or mdcat.pmdc.pk before making any plans around a specific date.
