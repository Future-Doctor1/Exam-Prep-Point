Hi, I'm the founder of Exam Prep Point

I'm currently a first-year MBBS student at Nishtar Medical University, Multan. I built Exam Prep Point because I remember exactly what it felt like to prepare for the MDCAT — scattered notes, outdated past papers, and no single place to practice chapter-by-chapter against the actual PM&DC pattern.

🎓 1st Year MBBS · Nishtar Medical University

Why I Built This

When I was preparing for MDCAT, I struggled to find one platform that combined chapter-wise practice, full-length mocks, and genuine past papers — all mapped correctly to the PM&DC curriculum weightage. Most resources were either outdated, scattered across different websites, or paywalled behind subscriptions that many students in Pakistan simply can't afford.

After getting into MBBS myself, I decided to build the resource I wished I'd had — using what I learned from my own MDCAT 2025 preparation and the mistakes I made along the way. Exam Prep Point is my attempt to make quality MDCAT 2026 preparation accessible to every aspiring doctor in Pakistan, regardless of their background or budget.

Our Mission

To give every MDCAT aspirant in Pakistan free, structured, and syllabus-accurate practice material — so preparation depends on effort, not access.
